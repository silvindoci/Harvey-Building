What to Look For

ENERGY STAR® Glazing

Save energy and money by making sure your windows have proper ENERGY STAR® glazing, which provides optimal thermal performance.


Fully-Welded Sash

A well-built tilt-in sash keeps your all your window elements securely in place while still allowing for easy cleaning.


Factory Calibrated Balances

When your block and tackle sash balances are calibrated in the factory, they never need adjustment or lubrication.


No Blue Tint

Make sure your vinyl extrusion does not have a bluish tint that is common with lower quality windows.


Multi-Chamber Frame

Air is a great insulator, which means that windows with more chambers slow the flow of cold air and better insulate your home.
